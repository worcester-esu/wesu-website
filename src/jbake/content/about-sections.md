  * East unit meets on Wednesdays in Claines. The leaders are James, Charlotte and Alex M.

  * West unit meets on Thursdays in Hallow. THe leaders are Charles, Tim, Luke, Amber, Alex W and James.
  * The Young Leader unit meets once per term to support [Young Leaders](about-youngleaders.html) in the District, the unit leaders are Siân, Sats, Chloe, Sean and Charles.
