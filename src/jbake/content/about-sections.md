Our five sections meet at various locations around Worcester on weekday evenings.

  * North unit meets on Tuesdays in Barbourne. The leaders are Stuart, Karen, Sophie and Billy.
  * South unit meets on Tuesdays in St Peters. The leaders are Siân, Steve, Dean, Alex M and Oliver.
  * West unit meets on Thursdays in Hallow. The leaders are Charles, James, Luke and Charlotte.
  * The Young Leader unit meets once per term to support [Young Leaders](about-youngleaders.html) in the District, the unit leaders are Siân, Sats, Chloe, Sean and Charles.
